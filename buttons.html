What are the different buttons in the game?

While Coins make their debut in Ship World, buttons are introduced in Mech World. These triggers can either clear a path to victory or land you in a tight spot depending on how you use them!

The Coins and Locked Blocks

To bypass a standard Locked Block, you'll need to hunt down a nearby coin. But be careful! If you die, you'll have to collect that coin again.

Orange Coins work differently: collecting one starts a ticking countdown. Once time runs out, the locked block snaps right back into place. Move fast, or you might have to collect the coin again, or even worse, get crushed by the block!

Gravity Weakeners

Touching this button causes you to jump much higher than normal. Pair it with a jump block to go even higher!

Gravity Strengtheners

Touching this button reduces your jump height. While it may sound like a drawback, it's incredibly useful for precision short-jumping beneath low-hanging hazards where a standard jump would send you flying into the ceiling of a block.

The Light Switch

Activating this button plunges almost the entire level into darkness! While navigating becomes a challenge, it's the only way to phase out the purple blocks blocking your path to the End Portal.

The Speedster

The speedster button boosts your velocity. Whether you need to clear a massive gap or sprint away from a rushing saw going down your path, this is the way for ultimate speed!
