Who are the Robos in the game?

Lil Robo

He is the starting character, and of course, the studio's main mascot! He is happy, innocent and charming!

Sunny Robo

Is there anything special about this fella? Maybe he is Lil Robo's lost brother... wait, robos have siblings?

Evil Robo

He once disappeared into a mysterious place... but so have many others who went there...

Green Robo

Her wisdom has always been a mystery, and so have her eyes...

Pirate Robo

He is the captain of the ship! That said, does anyone know which ship?

Iron Robo

No one knows whats hiding underneath his metal body... but it's for the best that no one knows...

Cakebot

Happy anniversary! Is he a cake, or is he a robot? Well, shocking news, he's both!

Cash Robo

He likes money, he has money, he wants money. He only knows money.

Vectorbot

Found in a factory, but who thought it was a good idea to bring him to life?!
