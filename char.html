Who are the characters and how can they be unlocked?

Characters are the Robos which you can unlock by fulfilling a certain objective!

Lil Robo

He is the starting character, and of course, the studio's main mascot! He is happy, innocent and charming!

Evil Robo

He is hiding somewhere in Green World... but if you find him and run away from him far enough, you will end up unlocking him!

Green Robo

She always has a peace of... processor? She will automatically unlock, when the all Green World Levels get GOLD rank!

Ice Robo

She is hiding somewhere... but she has not been spotted yet.

Lava Robo

He might be hiding in a secret volcano... completely isolated from the rest of the world... or is he?
