What's new in Roboquix v1.4.0?

1.5 New Worlds!

The all-new Desert World is here! Beware of cacti spikes that rush in the direction you're facing if you get too close. Balance yourself wisely around quicksand or you'll get sucked in! All this cool new content is spread over 4 big new levels — the hardest any robo has seen until now!

But wait, there's more! Green World has been split up! Levels 3 to 6 have been given their own new home: the Ship World. Meanwhile, Green World now has 2 new levels to keep it at 4 levels total. Don't worry — if you've played before, your progress will automatically transfer to Ship World!

Robo Rarities!

Robos have now been split into four exciting rarities based on how difficult they are to unlock: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. Rarities are mainly visual and define how hard it is to unlock a robo!

  • Common: Lil Robo, ??? (you will find out below...)
  • Rare: Evil Robo, Green Robo, ??? (no spoilers)
  • Epic: Iron Robo, Cakebot, ??? (hint: money, DONT WORRY NOT P2W!)
  • Legendary: ??? (just go a little lower!)

Four New Robos!

We've pretty much doubled the roster of playable robos since 1.3.0!

  • Sunny Robo (Common): Nothing special, pretty much a yellow copy-paste of Lil Robo. Unlock the speedy starter achievement to get him.
  • Pirate Robo (Rare):Get a Gold or better medal in the first (and at the time of writing this, only) subsection of Ship World to unlock this robo!
  • Cash Robo (Epic): The green greedy bot! Unlock the XP Collector achievement (reach XP Level 20) to get him.
  • Vectorbot (Legendary): The blueprint bot! Get Diamond medals for all 6 levels of the first (and for now, only) subsection of Mech World to unlock this robo.

Achievement System Overhaul!

Chase and Escaped is no longer obtainable. In its place, a new achievement called Terminal Velocity (complete Mech 1-5, formerly Level 11, in less than 15 seconds) is now the way to unlock Evil Robo! If you already had the old achievement, Evil Robo will still be unlocked for you and the old achievement will remain highlighted as green.

If you had the Golden achievement before this update, it's still unlocked and Green Robo remains available! Even better, if you already got a gold medal in the last 4 levels of Green World before this update, you automatically unlock Pirate Robo!

Other new stuff added.

  • New XP-based badges: Badges change based on your XP level and are displayed on the profile and the account selector screen alongside the username!
  • Animated robo faces: Robos' faces are now animated as they move, giving them more life!
  • Better world selection: The worlds selection screen now shows world names and the total number of stars and medals earned in each world.

Cleobo Engine Changes (0.7.2)

  • Notification fix: Fixed an issue where notifications sometimes didn't appear.
  • New level loader: A parser now loads Lua files and handles level and world data automatically.
  • World-based save structure: Progress is now saved per world instead of using a linear structure. Old progress automatically transfers!
  • New level logic: Better collisions and an overall smoother experience.

And other bug fixes and improvements!

Note

As stated above, progress from previous versions will be carried over automatically, so don't worry!
