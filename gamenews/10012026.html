What has changed?

Well, I'm back from the dead, and don't worry, I wasn't completely gone (to the two people who might actually read this, if you did do so, you're awesome, love you), and I was actually working on this game! Even though... I should focus on AS whoops!

Starting off, very important... Platformer 02 is now Roboquix!!

In terms of levels

  • Level 13 from the ice world has returned as Level 12 now! Though its not so ice-y anymore...

  • Green World has been split in half! We now have Levels 6-12 dedicated for Mechanics!

  • But don't worry... Green World is still here! Just from... Level 1-6 now only...

  • Added the diamond medal! Get zero deaths and complete the level in the gold medal range to get a diamond medal!

  • And... say hello to Iron Robo!

    • But that's not it! Something HUGE is now here...

    Introducing XP! When you complete a Level, depending on how much score you got and how many stars you got in a level, you now get XP for it! Its a nice way to show how good you are at the game!

    Hah... you thought that was the big thing... well got you wrong! Here is the best part...

    INTRODUCING ACCOUNTS!!!! Now, if you are switching over to a new device, and don't want to lose your progress, you can simply create an account, log into it and continue where you have left off!

    Even better, if you and, let's say, your siblings are playing on the same device, you can now have a separate account for each of them! This can be great to demonstrate who is the most skilled of you all!

    Thank you for reading this and have a good day!
