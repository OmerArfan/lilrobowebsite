Introducing the score system!(Beta)

The score system is a new feature in Platformer 02, where your skills FINALLY come to the test! It starts off with a default score of 100000, but several factors will help in lowering that score! The factors, to be exact, are...

TIME

THe main objective for a level that you play, is how quickly you finish the level. For every second you waste, or spend wisely... whatever... you lose 100 points.

MEDAL

Connected to how fast you complete the level, here's how many points you will lose when you get a medal:

GOLD: 5000 points

SILVER: 10000 points

BRONZE: 15000 points

Or, if you suck so badly you didn't even get a medal(no offense, you can try again): 25000 points
