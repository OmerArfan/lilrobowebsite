An update on the score system!

I've added(and am going to add) some new information related to the score system, and here are the changes!

Introducing tokens!

I have not developed them to that level yet, but tokens are an in-game collectible which, as of now, will only increase your score!(And this is also yet to be finalized, since I have not added them in all the levels yet.)

Stars?

Not yet finalized, but I will also be adding stars which will be an extra flex alongside your high score!

Other bug fixes:

  • Made sure data loss chance is reduced with a better save system!
  • Also removed the chance of getting a negative score, which, will lead to a gamebreaking bug that will get the game stuck at the level complete screen.
