What's new in roboquix?

Introducing The Cleobo Engine!

A new brain which changes how roboquix works on the inside! In-game too, you will notice how much better collisions have become! It isn't magic... it's Cleobo!

Introducing the Profile Page!

Ever wanted to look at your best stats easily? You can now check your gold and diamond medals, total stars and even an XP bar! Maybe one day you might even get some cool rewards... uh you read nothing...

Global Accessibility (Localization)

Ever wished the game would understand your native language? In roboquix, more than 10 languages are supported with almost full localization! (Some texts might still be left out).

User Interface Improvements!

  • New and improved transitions: Less likely to hang at the wrong places and makes the game feel a lot leaner!
  • Update Notifications: The game will notify you when a new version is available so you won't miss out!
  • Account Management: A separate page for login and registration now exists!
  • World Screen: World select menu improved slightly!

Gameplay & Level Polish!

  • Jump Blocks: Added indicator for easier identification!
  • Lighting: New circular vision when the lights are off!
  • Physics: Hit collision logic has improved as well!

And other bug fixes and improvements!
