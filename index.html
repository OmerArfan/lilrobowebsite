What is Roboquix?

Roboquix is a simple game where you move Lil Robo towards the end portal. You have to dodge spikes, jump through gaps, collect coins to open locked blocks, and run away from chasing saws. If you complete the level quickly, you will be rewarded a medal accordingly, and these will help you get a higher score, as long as you also try not to die while dodging obstacles. Not exactly like it, but it might give you an old feeling of nostalgia from the old platformer days!
