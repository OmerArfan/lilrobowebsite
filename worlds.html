What are the different worlds in the game?

As of right now, there are four worlds in the game. They are:

GREEN WORLD

In green world, you start off simple by getting to know the simple mechanics of the game!

SHIP WORLD

In ship world, you start to get into more dangerous territory... you now have to jump past saws, and collect coins to open locked blocks!

MECH WORLD

In this world, you get to know about fun new mechanics which alter the way you play in levels! From gravity strengtheners and weakeners to speedsters, these mechanics will test how well you can adjust to the abnormal.

DESERT WORLD

Welcome to the desert! Beware of cacti spikes that rush in the direction you're facing if you get too close, and balance yourself wisely around quicksand or you'll get sucked in!

Any other worlds?

For now, nope! However, in future updates the worlds list might get updated further and further!
