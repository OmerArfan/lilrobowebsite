What are the different worlds in the game?

Roboquix currently features four worlds. Each world introduces unique challenges to complete and content to master:

Green World

Your journey begins here! Green World helps you start off simple by getting to know the basic content of the game.

Ship World

Step into dangerous territory! In Ship World, you'll need to precisely leap past spinning saw blades while hunting down hidden coins to unlock locked blocks.

Mech World

Prepare to flip your perspective. This world introduces physics-altering mechanics that change how you play the game. From gravity modifiers that change how you jump to high-velocity speedsters, your skills will be tested on how well you can adjust to the abnormal.

Desert World

Welcome to the desert! Beware of cacti spikes that rush in the direction you're facing if you get too close, and balance yourself wisely around quicksand or you'll get sucked in! It is the hardest world any robo has ever seen yet!

Looking for more worlds?

The world selection doesn't stop here! Keep an eye out for future updates as I continue to introduce new worlds and expand levels in existing ones.
